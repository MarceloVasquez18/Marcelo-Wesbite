Smart Ball

Goal

To develop and use a technology that can measure a soccer player's kicking firmness

and overall performance with the soccer ball onto a mobile app.

Inspiration

I have a passion for soccer, although I do not have much time to play the sport anymore.

Building the Smart Ball will be a symbol of my love for soccer and for engineering.

Challenge
